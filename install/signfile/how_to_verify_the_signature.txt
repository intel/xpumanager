SignFile executable is signed with a detached signature. The signature is stored in SignFile.sig file.

To verify the signature with OpenSSL, perform these steps:
	1. Extract the certificates to certs.pem file:
	$ openssl pkcs7 -print_certs -inform der -in SignFile.sig > certs.pem

	2. Verify the signature:
	$ openssl smime -verify -in SignFile.sig -inform der -content SignFile -noverify certs.pem > /dev/null
	Expected output:
	Verification successful
